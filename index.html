About Us

SMV Tiffin & Masal Point is a local favorite in Vinukonda, Andhra Pradesh. We serve street-style evening snacks made fresh with authentic flavors. Visit with friends and family for crispy, spicy, and satisfying bites.

Muntha Masala

Street-style mix of puffed rice, groundnuts, onion, and masala spices.

Mirchi Bajji

Large green chilli dipped in besan batter and deep fried.

Bread Omelette

Toasted bread with fluffy masala omelette filling.

Egg Burji

Tomato Round Cutting Salad
